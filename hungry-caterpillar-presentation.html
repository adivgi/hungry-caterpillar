What is it?

A browser game where you guide a colorful caterpillar across a 25×20 grid, eating food to grow and score points — until you transform into a butterfly! 🦋

🐛

Snake Mechanics

Classic snake gameplay — grow by eating, avoid hitting yourself or walls

📖

Storybook World

Inspired by Eric Carle's "The Very Hungry Caterpillar" — warm, playful visuals

🍎

Eat & Grow

Collect fruits and junk food to reach 400 points and complete the journey

🦋

Win by Transforming

Hit 400 points and watch the caterpillar metamorphose into a butterfly

🌱

Tech Highlights

🖼️

Canvas 2D API

All rendering — caterpillar, food, grid, butterfly, sparkles — drawn entirely via canvas context calls

getContext('2d')
🔊

Web Audio API

OscillatorNode + GainNode chained for real-time sound synthesis. No audio files at all.

AudioContext
💾

localStorage

High score and top-5 leaderboard (fastest wins) persist across sessions with JSON serialization

localStorage.setItem
📱

Touch Support

touchstart + touchend events detect swipe direction. Works on any mobile browser.

touchend → delta
